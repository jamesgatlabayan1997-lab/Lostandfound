About Us

Mission

To provide a secure and user-friendly platform where students can easily report lost and found items by uploading images and details, helping reunite items with their rightful owners quickly and efficiently.

Vision

To become a reliable and smart lost and found solution that strengthens trust within the campus community and ensures that no valuable item goes unrecovered.
